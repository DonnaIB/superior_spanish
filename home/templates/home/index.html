About Us!

For those of you who don't already know, it all started with a language exchange, if you haven't heard of Dublin Intercambio go check it out. From there we have listened to our langage learning community and created more resources and support to help people learn Spanish.

Superior Spanish, is specifically aimed and intermediate learners who are trying to get to an advanced level. We know that there are a lot of resources and classes out there for beginners and lower intermediate, the A1 - B1 level. But what we realised is that there is less recourses out there to get beyond a B2 level, to get beyond that intermediate plateau and on to an advanced level.
